About Plateau Pro Flooring

40 Years of Dedicated Craftsmanship in Vancouver, BC

Our Journey: Four Decades of Excellence

Founded **40 years ago** in the vibrant city of Vancouver, BC, Plateau Pro Flooring began with a simple yet profound vision: to provide the highest quality flooring solutions with integrity and a commitment to customer satisfaction. What started as a small, passionate team has grown into a leading name in the local flooring industry, built on a foundation of trust, expertise, and a meticulous eye for detail.

Over the decades, we've honed our skills, embraced new technologies, and expanded our range of services to meet the evolving needs of our community. From classic hardwood installations to modern luxury vinyl planks and intricate tile designs, our experience spans every facet of residential and commercial flooring. We pride ourselves on understanding the unique demands of Vancouver's climate and architectural styles, ensuring every project is not only beautiful but also built to last.

Our success is not just about the floors we lay; it's about the relationships we build. We believe in clear communication, transparent pricing, and delivering on our promises. When you choose Plateau Pro Flooring, you're not just getting a new floor; you're investing in peace of mind, backed by **forty years of proven expertise** and a genuine passion for what we do.
